The development of innovative and educational applications has become a prominent trend in the technology industry, especially in the field of children's education. Our group embarked on a project focused on the concept development of a children's educational weather application through research, collaboration, and design efforts to produce a unique and valuable application for young learners.

APPLICATION GOALS

  •    Provide live accurate weather data
  •    Experience adapts to user's age/knowledge.
  •    Speech and Narration support
  •    Badges, awards, and unlock features
  •    Storyline learning tools
  •    Parental features
  •   Weekly status reporting
  •   Fun/easy-to-understand GUI

Petuina Beaudry

  •   Stay-at-home mom

BIOGRAPHY

Petunia is a firm believer in natural and holistic approaches to health and wellness. She tries to minimize her family's exposure to toxins and chemicals and is conscious of her environmental impact. She's also passionate about instilling these values in her children and teaching them to appreciate the beauty of nature.

GOALS

Petunia's main goal is to provide a healthy and nurturing environment for her family. She wants to raise her children to be mindful, compassionate, and resilient individuals who care about the world around them. She's interested in using technology to facilitate this process but is wary of apps and websites that promote consumerism or have a negative impact on children's well-being.

CHALLENGES

Petunia often feels overwhelmed and anxious about the state of the world and the impact of climate change on her children's future. She's always looking for ways to educate herself and her family about environmental issues and how to live more sustainably.

SCENARIO

Petunia is always looking for fun and educational activities to do with her child, Max. One day, while searching for weather-related educational tools, Lisa discovers Cato Weather on the App Store.


Excited by the prospect of using an app to teach her child about the weather, Petunia downloads the app and opens it up. Upon opening the app, Petunia is presented with a simple and intuitive user interface that guides her through customizing the application experience for her child.



Petunia decides to explore the app with Max, and they learn about different weather systems by reading about the weather around them to playing fun mini-games that progress in complexity as Max ages/interacts with the application.


After using the app for a while, Peruina is impressed with how engaging and educational it is. She plans to continue using the app regularly to teach Max about different weather systems and how they impact their daily lives.

USER GOALS

  •   Learn basic weather vocabulary, such as words for different types of precipitation, cloud formations, and wind patterns.

  •   Understand how weather changes throughout the day and different seasons, including how daylight hours and temperature vary over the year.

  •   Explore weather-related STEM concepts, such as the water cycle, air pressure, and atmospheric composition.

  •   Participate in interactive weather-related mini-games and quizzes, such as matching games, memory games, and jigsaw puzzles.

  •   Learn how to read and interpret weather maps and symbols, including basic knowledge of temperature scales, wind direction, and precipitation probability.

  •   Explore different locations around the world and learn about the weather patterns and seasons in each region.

  •   Understand the importance of weather safety, such as how to stay safe during a thunderstorm or extreme heat wave.

  •   Discover how different animals and plants adapt to different weather patterns, such as hibernation, migration, and flowering seasons.

COMPETITION


🌤 Weather Duck

A cartoon duck provides users with the local temperature and weather conditions and recommends how to prepare for the outdoors.



🌤 MarcoPolo Weather

Animated game for children to learn about different weather conditions. Users can select the temperature, time of day, and a variety of weather conditions and see how a selection of animated characters react to the different conditions.



🌤 Kid's Learning Quiz

Educational focus on matching words and pictures. users are shown a word and must choose from 4 different illustrations to make a match over a wide range of categories including weather.



🌤 K-5 Science For Kids

Self-guided learning app. Offers live-streamed workshops and interactive videos covering a wide variety of science-related topics, including weather.



🌤 Weather Farmer

Simulation game that calls on local weather within its game mechanics.



COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE


Cato Weather offers a more comprehensive and engaging experience for children compared to the competitors. While other weather-tracking apps like Weather Duck offer similar features, our app goes beyond basic weather tracking to include a range of interactive mini-games and learning experiences that teach children about weather patterns and systems in a fun and engaging way. In addition, our app will feature an innovative AR mobile weather learning game that can be played outdoors, allowing children to explore and learn about weather in a more interactive and immersive way.

REVENUE & MARKETING


Our primary revenue model is a pay-per-download model, which provides access to all current features of our app. With a one-time purchase price, users can enjoy access to a wide range of interactive mini-games, educational experiences, and real-time weather-tracking features in a fun and beautiful presentation.


We believe that quality education should be accessible to all, which is why we strive to keep our app's price point highly competitive. Our app provides value beyond just basic weather tracking, with a range of interactive learning experiences designed to engage and educate children of all ages.


In the future, we plan to use our brand recognition to launch other educational and enrichment apps, offering even more opportunities for learning and discovery. For older users who require the most accurate and up-to-date weather data, we plan to introduce a subscription model that opens new and exclusive features and games.


Our revenue model is designed to balance revenue generation with user satisfaction and value. With our app, parents and educators can feel confident that they are providing their children with a top-notch educational experience that is both affordable and engaging.


Marketing will focus on targeting both parents and children to successfully promote our paid educational weather app. Sponsored content on parent-oriented media platforms, such as podcasts and websites, will also be utilized to reach parents looking to enhance their child's screen time with educational content.


To target children, we plan to partner with popular YouTube channels that specialize in unboxing videos and other kid-friendly content. Through these partnerships, we hope to build buzz around Cato Weather and reach millions of children. Our messaging will emphasize the fun and gaming aspects of learning about the weather as well as the social benefits of the outdoor AR experience.


Overall, our marketing plan is designed to leverage both paid and organic channels to reach our target audience effectively. By targeting both parents and children and emphasizing the unique features and benefits of our app, we are confident that we can drive significant downloads and revenue over time.

CONCLUSION


The development of Cato Weather was a unique and innovative educational experience as it allowed us to think outside of the box when developing an application. Our focus was delivering a comprehensive and engaging weather education experience to children aged 3-13 years, the process allowed us to explore what is possible, and how it could be developed. With its possible range of interactive mini-games, learning experiences, and innovative AR mobile weather learning games, Cato Weather could offer a competitive advantage over its competitors, while its price point provides access to quality weather education that is affordable and accessible.


Starting with a well-defined user persona was integral to our app development process as it provided the team with a deep understanding of the target audience's needs, behaviors, and preferences. This enabled us to create a highly tailored and engaging user experience, making our app more competitive and ultimately resulting in a successful product.
