Goal

Produce high-quality images for Amazon’s affinity group Glamazon.

Pre-Production

The preparation for this project included research and the purchase of a new lens for my Canon SL3 camera. The lens in question is the Canon EMF 50MM f1.8, this lens was selected for its ability to create depth and bring distant subjects closer to the lens. I made this decision because my goal was to capture candid shots along the parade route along with obligatory staged team photos.


The night before the parade was spent preparing equipment, along with a brief meeting with the client to establish their requirements and expectations for the shoot. I was asked to focus on candid moments that convey the message of this year's Pride Parade theme “Together Again.” It was at this time the client also specified that I was to avoid getting any shots of children unless I was given permission from their parental figure.

The Shoot

The next day I checked in with my client at the Amazon YVR office and began the shoot.


My primary focus was to produce images that showcased movement, excitement, joy, and pride. I was able to accomplish this by navigating throughout the group, it was important during this movement I respected the safety guidelines set out by the Vancouver Pride Society. With safety in mind, I didn’t stay in a particular area too long, allowing me to capture honest and truly candid moments. It was also important to me that I captured shots of everyone participating, I did so by keeping a mental list of each person and ensuring that I was able to capture at least one shot of everyone. Throughout the event, I captured a total of 265 photos over the course of 3.5 hour event.
