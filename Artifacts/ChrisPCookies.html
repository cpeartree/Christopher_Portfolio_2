Goal

The goal of this project was to showcase my understanding of digital design principles, and the execution of mobile & web designs for a fictitious business. This introduction to UI/UX project leveraged both Figma and Adobe XD for prototyping and two different user testing systems.

User testing was an integral portion of both project phases to improve my designs and confirm that users were able to successfully complete assigned tasks.

Personas, Scenarios & User Flows

Before I could take pen to paper (or in my case, pencil to iPad) I spent some time developing User Personas & Scenarios to create a meaningful understanding of my users and implement that empathy into my design.

The perspective gained in the User Personas helped shape the User Flow that would outline the necessary steps a user would take. In this case, Phase I for Chris P Cookies included the launch of a new products and features.

Wireframes

For the web-based design of Chris P Cookies, 10 hand-sketched wireframes were created using Procreate on the iPad outlining the necessary pages required for users to interact with the website that will be later outlined in User Testing.

The second phase of Chris P Cookies put the focus on mobile design. Before implementation in Figma I produced a total of 5 wireframes outlining the introduction of the ratings & favourite systems.

Phase I - Desktop Prototype - Adobe XD

The first phase of Chris P Cookies was an introduction to the market with a broad selection of both cookies and merchandise available.

As outlined in the User Flow, the focus for this phase was to have users navigate throughout the design, and process an order for local delivery.

Here is the initial prototype.

Phase II - Mobile Prototype - Figma

After the successful launch of Chris P Cookies in the previous phase, I wanted to ensure the mobile user experience had the ability to favourite and provide ratings for product offerings.

building on the learnings from phase I, the design was optimized for mobile browsers, using Figma.

User Testing

In total there were 7 testers who were provided with the above Adobe XD Prototype through Useberry, and a total of 11 tests were completed for the mobile Figma prototype

The focus for this test was first to ensure that quick links allowed users to easily navigate the product offerings. The second was to check the viability and ease of use of a merchandise catalogue.

Users completed with an 80% successful completion rate on both tasks and only one user was unable to complete with findings suggesting the user bounced from the test after detouring from the provided task.
