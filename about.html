Navigating the intersections of design with a palette of creativity and adaptability. 🍐

My name is Christopher Poirier a recent New Media Design and Web Development graduate from BCIT, having earned my degree with distinction. My journey through four enriching terms has endowed me with a profound appreciation for the ever-evolving world of design.

With a foundation steeped in diverse retail verticals and an adeptness with both hardware and software technologies, I possess a multifaceted skill set poised to elevate any project. My commitment to collaborative synergy is unwavering, and I take immense pride in placing the user at the heart of every endeavor, always mindful of the ethos of inclusivity that colors my designs.

Beyond the realms of academia and professional pursuits, my passions include photography, fashion, literary exploration, alluring sci-fi realms, and the captivating realm of the Eurovision Song Contest. I find solace in the embrace of nature, often embarking on hikes that invigorate my spirit or discovering new cycling destinations that inspire new thoughts.

As I seek new horizons and experiences, I am eagerly anticipating the opportunity to contribute my proficiency to a summer internship in 2023. My journey so far has only deepened my enthusiasm for design, and I'm committed to channeling this enthusiasm into projects that captivate, inspire, and leave an indelible impact.

Thank you for joining me on this creative voyage. Let's craft meaningful experiences together.
